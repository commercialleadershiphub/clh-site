How to write a clear request

Use this quick model to keep it sharp:

  • Goal — what outcome do you want?
  • Audience — who will use it? role, seniority, region
  • Context — your product, sales motion, ACV, deal cycle
  • Constraints — must haves, compliance, systems
  • Success — how we will measure impact

Example: “We sell B2B SaaS, 20k ACV, EMEA mid market. Need a pricing governance playbook for partners. Goal: fewer ad hoc discounts. Success: margin up 3 points in Q4.”
