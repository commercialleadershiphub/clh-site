Lead Lifecycle & SLA

Problem

Leads bounce between teams. Aging skyrockets and reporting is noisy.

  • Clear states with entry/exit criteria and owner per state.
  • Response and routing SLAs; alerts on breach.
  • Weekly audit and root cause fixes.

Flow

  • New → MQL → SAL → SQL → Opp → Closed.

Metrics

  • Time‑to‑first‑touch; stage aging; acceptance rate; recycle reasons.
