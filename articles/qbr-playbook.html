QBR Playbook (Customer-Facing)

Prep, data pack, 60‑min agenda and crisp follow‑up that earns renewal and expansion.

Goal

Make the customer successful in the next 90 days and earn the right to expand. QBR ≠ show-and-tell. It is a working session with outcomes.

Prep checklist

  • Data pack sent 3 days prior (usage, outcomes, benchmarks)
  • Attendees confirmed incl. exec sponsor
  • 2–3 options for next quarter plan

Agenda (60 min)

  1. Outcomes last quarter
  2. What blocked value
  3. Plan & co‑ownership for next 90 days
  4. Risks and help needed

Artifacts

  • One‑pager success plan
  • Risk & dependency log
  • Expansion hypotheses with triggers

Follow‑up

Send recap within 24h. Book mid‑quarter check‑in now.
