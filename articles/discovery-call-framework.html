Discovery Call Framework & Notes

Questions, flow and note template that lead to clear next steps.

Objectives

  • Understand the problem, impact and priority
  • Test fit and identify stakeholders
  • Agree on a concrete next step

Flow (30–45 mins)

  1. Set agenda & time check
  2. Current state & pain
  3. Impact & value hypothesis
  4. Decision process & timeline
  5. Next step: workshop, pilot or executive intro

Prompts

  • What changes if you do nothing in 6–12 months?
  • Who owns the outcome? Who can say no?
  • How have you tried to solve it before?
  • What does success look like this quarter?
