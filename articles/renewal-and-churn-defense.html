Renewal & Churn Defense

Signals, save plays and escalation comms that work.

Problem

Churn is spotted too late. No playbook, random escalations, last‑minute discounts.

What good looks like

  • Risk signals defined (usage, contacts, tickets, exec sponsor).
  • Save plays by root cause (value, fit, budget, champion left).
  • Weekly risk review with actions and executive support.

Steps

  1. Define risk score and thresholds.
  2. Map plays to causes, with email/call scripts.
  3. Run a weekly “Save Room”.

Templates

  • Risk scorecard; Save play checklist; Exec brief email.

Metrics

  • Logo/Gross/Net retention.
  • Time‑to‑risk detection.
  • Save rate by cause.

Risks

  • Discounts replacing value → require counters before price moves.
