Value Realization Reviews

EBR pack, KPIs and ROI narrative you can reuse.

Problem

Quarterly reviews drift into status updates. No hard value proof, so expansions stall.

What good looks like

  • One deck: goals, outcomes, proof, roadmap asks.
  • Two stories: business impact and user stories.
  • Clear next steps: expansion hypothesis + pilot.

Steps

  1. Re‑state original goals and KPIs.
  2. Show before/after graphs and customer quotes.
  3. Propose expansion with modeled ROI.

Templates

  • EBR deck outline; ROI calc sheet; next‑step email.

Metrics

  • EBR‑to‑expansion conversion.
  • Attendance of exec sponsor.

Risks

  • Feature dump → keep to outcomes and proof.
