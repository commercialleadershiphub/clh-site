Proof-of-Concept Plan

Entry/exit, success metrics and resourcing in one page.

Problem

POCs sprawl, eat resources and end with “let’s revisit next quarter”.

What good looks like

  • Business outcome stated up front and measured weekly.
  • Two‑to‑four week window; clear exit criteria; no free services creep.
  • Small team with named time commitment.

Steps

  1. Agree business metric and target (e.g., +20% win‑rate).
  2. Pick 1–2 use cases; define dataset and environment.
  3. Staffing: roles, hours/week, availability.
  4. Exit criteria and procurement next steps.

Template

  • Goal | Use cases | Data/env | Team & hours | Exit criteria | Next steps

Metrics

  • POC duration adherence.
  • Conversion rate to paid.
  • Services hours burned vs. plan.

Risks

  • Undefined “success” → write it in numbers.
  • Unbounded dataset → freeze a snapshot.
