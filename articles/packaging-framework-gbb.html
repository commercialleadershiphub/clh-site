Packaging Framework (Good/Better/Best)

Fences, value metrics and migration paths that make sense.

Problem

Bundles grow randomly; quoting is slow; customers can’t self‑select.

What good looks like

  • Three packages with clear fences and one value metric.
  • Upgrade path and entitlements policy.
  • Sales guide: who gets what and why.

Steps

  1. Pick value metric (users, volume, units).
  2. Fence by capability and scale.
  3. Define migration rules and price deltas.

Templates

  • GBB matrix; Entitlements policy; Sales guide.

Metrics

  • ARPU; attach rates; downgrade rate.

Risks

  • Too many SKUs → hold at three core.
