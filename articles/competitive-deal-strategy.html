Competitive Deal Strategy Checklist

Identify traps, shape criteria and win the narrative against strong rivals.

Win plan

  • Trap competitors with criteria that favor you
  • Mobilize a champion; enable them to sell
  • De‑risk with pilot or phased plan

Plays

  • Reference call early
  • Mutual plan written by the customer
  • Executive sponsor alignment

Countering status quo

Quantify cost of delay; show fast path to value.
