Ops

Forecast Accuracy System

Operating model to get within ±5–8%: hygiene rules, cadence, inspection and dashboards.

Target state

  • Reliable forecast within ±5–8% by week 6 of the quarter
  • Single source of truth: CRM + three dashboards
  • Manager inspection every week; no side spreadsheets

Hygiene rules (always on)

  • Every opp has next step + date
  • Close date within 90 days unless Stage 1–2
  • Primary contact + role set; exec sponsor for >$50k
  • Stage exit criteria met with attached proof

Cadence

WhenAudienceFocusArtifacts
WeeklyAE + ManagerSlips, risks, new pipelineForecast view, Risk log
Bi‑weeklyLeadersCoverage & conversion trendFunnel dashboard
MonthlyExecScenario delta vs. planExec summary, actions

Inspection prompts

  • What changed since last week, and why?
  • Which 3 deals are most at risk? What proof removes the risk?
  • Is time‑in‑stage healthy vs. benchmark?
  • What is the plan to create new pipeline for next quarter?
