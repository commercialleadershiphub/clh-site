Budget that sales can actually deliver

Headcount planning that supports growth without breaking the model.

8 min read · Updated 2025-08-12

Tie headcount to productive capacity, not just ARR targets. Start from funnel math: coverage, conversion and cycle times. Model sensitivity so you can defend headcount asks.

Sequence roles: first customer value (AEs/AMs), then pipeline engines (BDRs/Marketing), then enablers (Ops/Enablement). Delay management hires until the span of control breaks.

Bake ramp profiles into quota and cost. A realistic ramp curve protects your CAC and prevents surprise under‑performance late in the quarter.

Hold a monthly capacity review: hiring progress, ramp status, productivity by cohort and plans to close gaps.
