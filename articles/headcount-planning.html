Headcount Planning

Budgets, territory design and hiring cadence that scales without breaking productivity.

Capacity model

Start from revenue goal → coverage model → roles required. Include ramp times and productivity curves.

Territory design

  • Even opportunity per rep (ACV × account count)
  • No overlaps; clear rules of engagement
  • Rebalance quarterly

Hiring cadence

Hire ahead of need by ramp time. Example: 3‑month SDR ramp, 5‑month AE ramp.
