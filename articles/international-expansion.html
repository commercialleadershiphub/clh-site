International Expansion

Market selection, entry motions and risk controls for first wins in new regions.

Where to play

  • Size the prize: TAM, ACV, buying cycles
  • Fit: product, compliance, language, support hours
  • Entry friction: data residency, tax, payments

Entry sequence

  1. Signals — inbound demand, partner pull, customer expansion
  2. Pilots — lighthouse accounts with success plans
  3. Scale — hire first AE/CSM; localize marketing; add partners

Risk controls

  • Residency & privacy — SCCs/DPA, region-specific data hosting
  • Billing entity & tax — invoicing, VAT/GST, currency
  • Support — coverage hours, language, escalation
