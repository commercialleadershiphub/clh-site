Hiring your first two AEs

Scorecards and interview loops that raise the bar from day one.

8 min read · Updated 2025-08-12

Your first two AEs set the cultural and commercial bar. Define a hard scorecard across discovery, deal strategy, execution hygiene and team behaviour.

Run a tight loop: screen (30 min problem solving), working session on a real account, references, and a final panel focused on collaboration.

Onboard with a 30‑60‑90 plan: pipeline generation, first deals, expansion motion. Review weekly with objective evidence, not anecdotes.

Coach towards the scorecard and document what great looks like. This is the seed for your enablement library.
