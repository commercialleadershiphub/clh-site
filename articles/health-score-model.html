Customer Health Score Model

Simple, explainable score that predicts churn and expansion and drives actions.

Design principles

  • Explainable — 5–7 factors, no black boxes
  • Lead indicators — usage, adoption, value
  • Actionable — playbook per bucket

Model

FactorWeightDefinition
Usage30%Weekly active users vs. seats
Outcomes20%KPIs achieved
Support10%Time to resolve
Executive sponsor20%Engaged quarterly
Financial20%Invoices on time

Actions

  • Red → exec call, success plan, weekly check‑ins
  • Amber → training, feature enablement
  • Green → expansion hypothesis
