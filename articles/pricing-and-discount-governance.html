Pricing & Discount Governance

Guardrails and deal‑desk flow that protect margin without slowing deals.

Principles

  • Simple bands by segment and term
  • Give–get framework (value for every concession)
  • Fast approvals with clear ladders
  • No surprises at renewal

Policy

SegmentList discountMax AEManagerVPCFO
SMB0–15%10%15%20%—
Mid‑market0–20%12%18%25%30%+
Enterprise0–25%15%20%28%35%+

Anything above VP requires CFO approval + give–get items.

Give–get menu

Customer gets ↓

  • Extra discount
  • Flex billing
  • Pilot credits

We get ↑

  • 2‑year term / multi‑year
  • Case study + logo
  • Pre‑paid annually

Deal‑desk flow

  1. AE submits quote + justification + give–get
  2. Auto‑checks policy; routes to approver
  3. Finance reviews pocket margin
  4. Approval or counter with alternative structure
