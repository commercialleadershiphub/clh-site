Pricing & Discount Governance

Protect margin without slowing deals. Clear rules, fast exceptions.

Goals

  • Keep average selling price and gross margin healthy.
  • Speed up approvals with a simple, visible flow.
  • Make trade‑offs explicit with give‑get rules.

Guardrails (publish these)

  • Floor price per SKU/segment.
  • Standard discount bands: 0–10, 11–20, 21–30, 31+ percent.
  • Term limits: payment terms, ramp, and free trials.
  • Non‑standard items need approval (custom features, bespoke SLAs).

Approval ladder

  • 0–10 percent: manager OK.
  • 11–20 percent: sales director.
  • 21–30 percent: CRO + finance.
  • 31+ percent or below floor: CEO.

Give‑get rules (examples)

  • Discount > 15 percent → 24‑month term.
  • Free pilot → reference and case study on success.
  • Logo lockout → higher price or multi‑year commitment.

Deal desk flow (fast track)

  1. Rep submits summary: price, discount, term, risk, EV, and why it wins.
  2. Finance sanity‑checks margin; legal lists redlines.
  3. Decision in 24 hours or auto‑escalate.
  4. Write‑back to CRM fields for reporting.

Reporting

  • Discount by segment and stage won/lost.
  • Deals below floor and reason codes.
  • Give‑get compliance and pilot conversion.

Anti‑patterns

  • Silent specials that never reach the dashboard.
  • Approvals in Slack with no system record.
  • Give without get. Write rules and enforce them.

Publish the rules. Apply them weekly. Exceptions are fine, as long as they are visible.
