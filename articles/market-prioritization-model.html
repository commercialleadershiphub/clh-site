Market Prioritization Model

TAM/SAM, ease-of-entry and a practical risk score.

Model

  • Size (TAM/SAM), growth, ACV potential, ease (legal, language, infra), competition.

Steps

  1. Score markets 1–5 per factor.
  2. Weight by strategy.
  3. Pick top 3; sanity‑check with advisors and customers.

Output

  • Ranked list; first‑year goals; hiring plan; risk log.
