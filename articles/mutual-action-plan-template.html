Mutual Action Plan (MAP) Template

Dates, owners, risks and comms that keep deals moving.

Problem

Deals stall because only the seller has a plan. No shared dates, no customer owners, and legal/security join too late.

What good looks like

  • One page, shared with the buyer on day 1 of evaluation.
  • Milestones from today to go‑live with named customer owners.
  • Risk log with counters and a weekly 15‑min check‑in.

Steps

  1. List milestones: eval start, security, legal, pilot, exec sign, go‑live.
  2. Assign owners on both sides; agree dates and proof of done.
  3. Flag risks early (data, SSO, procurement) and add counters.
  4. Share link (doc/sheet) and review weekly.

Template

  • Milestone | Date | Seller owner | Customer owner | Status | Risk/notes
  • Weekly notes: decisions, blockers, next actions.

Metrics

  • Cycle time variance vs. plan.
  • # milestones with named customer owners.
  • Slips caught ≥2 weeks ahead.

Risks

  • Over‑engineered MAP → keep to one page.
  • “We’ll fill it later” → complete live on the call.
