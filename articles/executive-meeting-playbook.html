Executive Meeting Playbook

How to prep, run and follow up on CXO meetings that move deals forward — narrative, proofs and asks.

Outcomes

  • Clarity on executive priorities and success measures
  • Agreement on a concrete plan with owners and dates
  • Elevated champion with air cover to progress the deal

Preparation (T‑7 days)

  1. Brief — who, roles, current state, risks, competitors
  2. Hypothesis narrative — 3 slides: Why change, Why now, Why us
  3. Proofs — one customer story per priority; quantified outcomes
  4. Asks — decisions required (sponsor, access, resources, timeline)

Agenda (50 mins)

  1. Context & goals (5)
  2. Their priorities & constraints (10)
  3. Value narrative + proof (15)
  4. Plan, risks & options (15)
  5. Decisions & next steps (5)

Narrative structure

SlideContentNotes
1: SituationExternal drivers + internal goalsUse their language and metrics
2: FrictionWhere value is leaking todayQuantify with their data
3: Better stateWhat good looks like in 6–12 monthsLink to OKRs
4: HowPhased plan and responsibilitiesShow low-risk path
5: ProofRelevant customer storyOutcome + timeline + quote
6: AskDecisions and datesLimit to 2–3 clear asks

Follow‑up (T+24h)

  • One‑page recap with agreed outcomes and owners
  • Calendar holds for next workshop / pilot kick‑off
  • Risk log + actions (internal and customer)
