MEDDICC Qualification Checklist

Plain-English prompts, stage exits and a simple R/A/G scoring model.

When to use

Use MEDDICC for all deals above your strategic threshold (e.g. >$25k ACV) from Stage 2 onward. Keep the artifact in CRM so managers can inspect asynchronously.

Prompts

M – Metrics

  • What is the quantified outcome we will deliver?
  • What baseline do they have today?
  • What KPI moves and by how much?

E – Economic buyer

  • Who signs and owns the budget?
  • What do they care about this quarter?
  • How will they evaluate ROI?

D – Decision criteria

  • Security, integrations, services — rank by weight.
  • What must be proven to advance?

D – Decision process

  • Steps, approvers, procurement, legal.
  • Timeline with calendar holds.

I – Identify pain

  • What breaks if they do nothing?
  • Who feels the pain day to day?

C – Champion

  • Influence, access, and personal win.
  • What do we give them to sell internally?

C – Competition

  • Status quo, internal build, or vendors.
  • Trap-setting: where are we better?

Stage exits

StageExit proof
DiscoveryPain quantified, champion identified, next workshop booked
ValidationMutual plan with dates; decision criteria documented
ProposalEconomic buyer briefed; pricing structure agreed
CommitPaper process started; redlines, order form, start date

R/A/G score

Score each letter 0–2 (red/amber/green). Any red in EB, Champion or Criteria blocks the commit forecast. Use this to steer manager coaching — not to police reps.
