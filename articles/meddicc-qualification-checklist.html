MEDDICC Qualification Checklist

Use this as a living pre‑close checklist. Keep it visible in your CRM.

Metrics

  • What measurable impact will the buyer achieve? (Revenue, cost, risk)
  • Baseline and target documented?
  • Proof points ready for the economic buyer?

Economic Buyer

  • Who signs? When did we last speak with them?
  • What do they care about in one sentence?
  • What can kill the deal from their view?

Decision Criteria

  • Technical and commercial criteria listed and ranked?
  • Winning edge stated in one line for each criterion?

Decision Process

  • Steps, owners, dates, and legal/procurement mapped?
  • Mutual plan shared and accepted?

Identify Pain

  • What is broken today? What happens if they do nothing?
  • Cost of delay quantified?

Champion

  • Who loses if we slip? What do they need from us this week?
  • Do they have political capital with the EB?

Competition

  • Named rivals and status. Our counter‑moves.
  • Plan if the buyer “goes dark”.

Stage exits (add to your CRM)

  • Qualified: Pain validated, EB named, next step agreed.
  • Proposal: Decision process mapped, mutual plan in draft.
  • Commit: EB met, criteria met, mutual plan in execution.

RAG scoring (quick sanity check)

  • Green: All seven letters have evidence.
  • Amber: One to two weak areas; mitigation in plan.
  • Red: Three plus unknowns; pause or re‑qualify.

How to use weekly

  • Attach this as a checklist to top 10 EV deals.
  • Review two letters per week in 1:1s; rotate.
  • Document gaps and single next step per gap.

Qualification is not a form. It is a habit. Keep it visible.
