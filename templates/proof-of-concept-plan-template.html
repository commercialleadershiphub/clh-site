Proof-of-Concept (POC) — Companion Template

Use on kickoff; keep it short, numeric and owned on both sides. Print or save as PDF.

How to use

  • Fill this live with the customer on the kickoff call (screen share).
  • Time-box to 2–4 weeks; freeze a data snapshot; ≤2 use cases.
  • Instrument success on day 1; hold a 20-minute weekly review.
  • Pre-agree the commercial path on pass; decide next step on fail (no soft extends).

Tip: maintain a change log for any scope/data shifts with impact and approvals.
