Proof-of-Concept Plan Template

Copy this one-pager into your doc/sheet. Keep it short, numeric and owned on both sides.

How to use

  • Fill this live with the customer on the kickoff call (screen share).
  • Time-box to 2–4 weeks. Freeze scope to ≤2 use cases and a data snapshot.
  • Instrument success on day 1. Hold a 20-minute weekly review.
  • Pre-agree the commercial path on pass; decide the next step on fail (no soft extends).

Tip: keep a running “change log” when scope or data moves, with impact and approvals.

POC Charter (copy-ready)

Outcome & metric

  • Goal: e.g., “Reduce false positives by 25% in 3 weeks.”
  • Metric & target: precision +8 pts at same recall
  • Baseline: current = 0.71 precision
  • Why now: board date / SLA / cost pressure

Scope & data

  • Use cases (≤2): UC1, UC2
  • Data snapshot: source, rows, date range, fields
  • Out of scope: integrations, custom UI, edge cases…
  • Environment: vendor cloud / customer sandbox; access rules

Success criteria

  • SC1: metric ≥ threshold
  • SC2: process time ≤ target
  • SC3: stakeholder sign-off received

Risks & counters

  • Data access → assign data owner; sample first
  • Scope creep → change log + approvals
  • Capacity → realistic hours; substitutes

Exit & commercial path

  • Decision checkpoint date: Week 3 Friday 14:00
  • On pass: security/DP redlines owner, PO/approval dates
  • On fail: stop or next experiment (1 sentence)
